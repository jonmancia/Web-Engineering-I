My Favorite Gadget

A Raspberry Pi is a credit card-sized computer. Hobbysts use them for small projects such as learning to code, play retro video games, and even to install home security cameras! There's even a smaller version of the Raspberry Pi called the Raspberry Pi Zero which is just under 2 inches long and under an inch wide. The computer can boot an entire open source operating system called Raspbian. Click on the image to learn more!
